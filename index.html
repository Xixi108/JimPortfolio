04 — About

THE ARTIST

I'm Jim Mar Zarriz, a Graphic Artist with over 9 years of professional experience in graphic design, illustration, photo editing, and digital content creation, working with both local and international clients.

For the past two years I've worked full-time as a Graphic Artist for a company serving multiple websites in the UK gambling industry, crafting promotional graphics, banners, and digital assets that drive marketing campaigns. Earlier roles as a photo editor, design artist, and illustrator built my foundation in visual communication, branding, and creative problem-solving — all backed by deep proficiency in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

Outside of client work, I'm always sketching, sculpting, painting, or designing T-shirts and vector art for fun — and when I'm not at the desk, you'll usually find me cycling or skateboarding.
